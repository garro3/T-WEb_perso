Our story

Founded in 2025 by Joe from Breaking Bad, CacaCar was born from a simple idea: to make mobility more accessible and enjoyable while reusing vehicles considered obsolete. Since our beginnings, we have helped our customers find the ideal vehicle, whether it's a first purchase, a favorite, or a practical solution for everyday life.

We believe that a car is not just a means of transport, but also a promise of freedom, adventure, and new experiences.

Our values

Trust

We place transparency at the heart of our relationship with our customers, for simple and not reliable choices.

Innovation

We are against any technological advancement.

Closeness

We remain attentive to your needs and support you at every stage of your journey. Except in case of breakdown.
